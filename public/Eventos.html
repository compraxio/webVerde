Eco-Fair Amazonia

Celebrating sustainable innovation and rainforest conservation through community-led green trade initiatives.

description About the Event

The Eco-Fair Amazonia is a premier gathering of eco-conscious entrepreneurs, conservationists, and sustainable business leaders dedicated to the preservation of the Amazon basin. This event showcases groundbreaking green technologies, artisanal forest products, and sustainable agriculture techniques that prove economic prosperity can go hand-in-hand with environmental stewardship.

Join us for three days of interactive workshops, business matchmaking sessions, and the celebrated 'Green Marketplace' where local communities present their non-timber forest products to international buyers. Our mission is to strengthen the 'Green Economy' network across the Pan-Amazon region.
